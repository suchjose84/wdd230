Target Audience

The target audience of Panahon Weather web site is described below:

  • Who: High School Students, Teachers, Employees from all industries, College Students, and others who are interested to know the weather and have a smart phone.
  • Age: 14 years old and above.
  • Income: Income levels will be between (Php)10,000 and above monthly.
  • Motivation: Improve weather awareness. Improve disaster preparedness and survivability.
